Technology and Ethics

The key learning objectives for this simulation are:

  • Be able to distinguish biased sources from authoritative sources in discussions about technology.
  • Be able to identify poorly supported or opinionated statements regarding technology-based ethical issues.

As a group, you and some other classmates have been assigned to write a position paper on information technology and ethics. Your group decides that each member will write a brief paragraph on a proposed topic and from those topics the group will determine which one will be developed into a full paper

When you are presenting a position, you need to be careful to follow certain practices, like avoiding using opionions instead of verified facts.

To help you construct the most persuasive argument, your instructor has posted a quick tutorial to remind you of the best techniques to use.
